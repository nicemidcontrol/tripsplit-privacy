TripSplit ("the App") helps users split group expenses and view settlement summaries. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

## 1) Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data you create in the app (stored on your device)
The App stores trip and expense data locally on your device (on-device storage). We do not require account registration.

### 1.2 Data collected automatically (third-party services)
The App uses third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services may automatically collect:
- Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID / app instance identifiers)
- IP address (may be used to estimate general location)
- App activity and interaction data (e.g., app launches, taps, ad interactions)
- Diagnostics and performance data (e.g., crash/performance signals)

## 2) How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Provide and improve app functionality
- Understand app usage and improve user experience (analytics)
- Show ads and prevent fraud/abuse (advertising)

## 3) Sharing of Information
We may share information with third-party service providers that help operate the App, including:
- Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for advertising
- Firebase Analytics for analytics and measurement

These providers process data according to their own policies.

## 4) Sharing Features (User-Initiated)
If you choose to share a summary using your device's share feature (e.g., messaging or email apps), the content is sent to the app/service you select. We do not receive this shared content.

## 5) Data Retention
Trip and expense data remains on your device unless you delete it in the app or uninstall the app. Automatically collected analytics/ads data is retained as determined by the third-party providers.

## 6) Security
We take reasonable measures to protect information. Data transmitted by third-party SDKs may be encrypted in transit (e.g., via HTTPS/TLS).

## 7) Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8) Your Choices
- You can reset or limit your Android Advertising ID in device settings.
- You can stop data collection by uninstalling the App.

## 9) Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
